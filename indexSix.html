Summary

The implication of this study is also prevalent from the technology change which offers benefits to the economic output of the small businesses in Kuala Lumpur. In respect of the technology change, there will be seamless transfer of information through shared electronic files and networked computer, increases the efficiency of business processes such as documentation, data processing and other back-office functions (e.g. organizing incoming orders and preparing invoices). Internet-based interaction and real-time communication can reduce information asymmetries between small businesses and build closer relationship among trading partners (Moodley, 2002). The adopters of e-commerce tend to reduce transaction costs, increase transaction speed and reliability, and extract maximum value from transactions in their value chains (OECD, 2002a).

This study is important as it will bring understanding on the efficacy of the small businesses through the launch of a free website with free hosting (the website will be running, completed and administered by the candidate). This free website will allow any small businesses to access 24/7 with the ability to facilitate safe and secure transactions.

Later, the knowledge from the data collection in respect of the free website (with the approval by the respondents) can be used to reach new customers, market expansion, improvement of service quality, product development and innovation.
